We believe in happy cows

At Meadowbrook Farms, we believe that happy cows produce the best milk and cheese. That's why we go to great lengths to ensure that our cows are have have plenty of space to roam and graze on lush pastures and are healthy, happy, and cared for. We love our cows, you'll love our cheese and milk.
